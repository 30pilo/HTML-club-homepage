JOIN US, BE PART Of US, CHOICE IS YOURS…
The APU Chinese Language and Culture Society cordially invites your participation in this recruitment campaign and works to realise your aspirations. The Diabolo team, the Debate team, the Chinese Orchestra team, the Dance team, and finally the Media team are the five teams that make up APU CLCS. We are eager to welcome you to the university campus as the pandemic begins to heal so that you can start a fresh new chapter of university life. We invite you to get along with us and make wonderful moments with us as a student who enjoys Chinese traditional cultures and shares our passion.
Please feel free to register by scanning the QR Code on the poster or by clicking the registration link in our website, as we take registrations at all times.
– Member Fee: RM35 –
Value Package (member fee + club shirt): RM35
Lite Package (member fee): RM8
The membership fee is an upfront cost. Club shirts can be picked up at our physical recruitment station during registration, or you can choose to pick them up when you get back to campus.
